About PP

This Pani puri is originated in the ancient kingdom of Magadha (now a part of Bihar) in India . These ancient Pani Puri were also called 'Phulki'.

Pani_puri

What is the Ingredients of pani puri ?

Panipuri consists of a round hollow puri (a deep-fried crisp flatbread), filled with a mixture of flavored water (known as imli pani), tamarind chutney, chili powder, chaat masala, potato mash, onion, or chickpeas.(Source: Wikipedia)
